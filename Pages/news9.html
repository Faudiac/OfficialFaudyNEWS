"Death of Soccer Legend -Pele-"

December 29th of 2022, after the World Cup 2022 ended the world fell into a shock hearing the Death of the greatest Soccer player in football history "Pele". Pele Dies at the age of 82 in, Albert Einstein Israelite Hospital, São Paulo, State of São Paulo, Brazil. When the world of football began he, Pele was the star and the king of Football.

Edson Arantes do Nascimento, better know from nick name Pele' was a proffesional footballer. He was regared as one of the greatest players of all time (GOAT) thus labbelled as "The greatest" by Fifa. He has won many trophies and made many records.

After the death of Pele the world was in a state of shock and disbelief because the GOAT and king of football passed away. People also call him the creator of football. But today we say RIP Pele Goat (Greatest of all time) of football.
